Alex J H Fedorec

Group leader

I am a new group leader (assistant professor/lecturer) in the Department of Cell & Developmental Biology, Division of Biosciences at University College London, UK. My current research involves applying computational and mathematical techniques to understand and engineer new biological systems. I am particularly interested in biotherapeutic and bioindustrial applications of biological engineering. Recently I have focused on creating stable and predictable synthetic microbial communities in complex environments, such as the gastrointestinal tract.

I was previously a Research Fellow in the Computational Systems and Synthetic Biology group in the Division of Biosciences at University College London, UK. I have a background in computer science and industrial experience as a software engineer at Research in Motion (now BlackBerry), as well as being a proficient molecular and microbiology wetlab scientist.
