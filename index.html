Interdisciplinary & Collaborative

Mixing biology with mathematics, engineering and computing

As with any biological engineering group, we take the Richard Feynman quote "What I cannot create, I do not understand" as scripture. While developing a greater understanding of existing biological systems, we also aim to develop new systems that can help to solve some of the key challenges facing the world. To do this we combine wet and dry lab work to explore three inter-related areas: microbial communities, self-organisation, and quantitative methods.
